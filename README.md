# 📊 Customer Segmentation Analysis Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard that analyzes customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and marketing responses to identify valuable customer segments and generate actionable business insights.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project uses the **Customer Personality Analysis** dataset to explore customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and campaign responses. The dashboard helps businesses understand customer characteristics and supports data-driven marketing decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
- Identify customer spending patterns.
- Compare customer income across demographic groups.
- Evaluate marketing campaign responses.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for business insights.

# 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

### 📌 Average Income by Marital Status
Compares average annual income across different marital status groups.

### 📌 Income vs Total Spending by Marital Status
Analyzes the relationship between customer income and total spending.

### 📌 Customer Distribution by Marital Status
Displays customer distribution using a pie chart.

### 📌 Average Income by Education Level
Shows income variation across education levels.

### 📌 Total Spending by Education Level
Highlights which education groups contribute the highest spending.

### 📌 Campaign Response by Education Level
Visualizes marketing campaign responses using a funnel chart.

---

# 💡 Business Insights

- Married customers form the largest customer segment.
- Customers with Graduation and Master's education contribute the highest spending.
- Higher income customers generally spend more.
- Graduation-level customers show the highest campaign response.
- Education level has a significant impact on customer purchasing behavior.
